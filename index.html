Secure Laptop Donation Management System

A workflow system for IT sanitization verification, CSR approval, donation completion, and record export

Overview

This project presents a Cybersecurity-Based Laptop Donation Framework implemented through a web-based system. It is designed to mitigate data privacy risks when organizations donate used laptops.

Many organizations rely on simple formatting or factory reset, which does not permanently remove sensitive data. This system ensures secure sanitization, verification, risk classification, and approval before donation.

Cybersecurity Framework

  1. Risk Identification – Identify potential data leakage risks from donated devices
  2. Secure Data Sanitization – Use approved tools such as DBAN or Blancco following NIST standards
  3. Verification – Validate sanitization using wipe logs and certificates
  4. Risk Classification – Classify devices as Low, Medium, or High risk based on evidence
  5. Approval Control – CSR manager reviews and approves or rejects laptops
  6. Audit & Traceability – Maintain records of approvals, rejections, and donation details
  7. Compliance – Align with cybersecurity standards such as NIST SP 800-88

This framework is enforced through the system to ensure secure, traceable, and compliant laptop donation processes.

System Workflow

Inventory Upload → Secure Sanitization → Risk Assessment → CSR Approval → Donation Completion → Audit & Record Export
